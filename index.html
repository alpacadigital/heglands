Questions

Before you ask…

Are estimates really free?

Yes — completely. We'll come out, walk the property with you, talk through options and give you a written estimate. No cost, no obligation, no pressure.

What areas do you serve?

Rochester, Minnesota and the surrounding communities. If you're not sure whether you're in our area, just reach out — odds are good we already work in your neighborhood.

When should I book my project?

The earlier the better — Minnesota's building season is short and the schedule fills fast. Reaching out in winter or early spring usually means better timing options.

Do you handle both design and installation?

Yes. We design around how you actually want to use the space, then our own crew builds it — one point of contact from the first sketch to the final cleanup.

What materials do you use?

Trusted, proven products — including Willow Creek and Bogert paver stone — along with natural limestone, fieldstone and boulders selected for each project.
